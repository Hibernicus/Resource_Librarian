W/L2.3; Write a range of texts, including extended written documents, communicating information, ideas and opinions effectively and persuasively.
W/L2.3a; Present information/ideas concisely, logically and persuasively;
W/L2.3b; Present information on complex subjects clearly and concisely;
W/L2.3c; Use a range of writing styles for different purposes;
W/L2.3d; Use a range of sentence structures, including complex sentences, and paragraphs to organise written communication effectively.
W/L2.3e; Punctuate written text using commas, apostrophes and inverted commas accurately;
W/L2.3f; Ensure written work is fit for purpose and audience, with accurate spelling and grammar that support clear meaning in a range of text types.
